Small History Of Diroga

Posted November 11, 2020

In this section we'll discover how Diroga was first founded

Before Diroga was even concieved, a person who goes by 立MEGA VIRUS became obsessed with an IO game known as arras.io, an unofficial sequal to another IO game. After playing the game for some time, he joined the discord server for it, where he met a user known as Dooky. Though they hated each-other in the game, once they talked a bit on discord, they became friends. They discussed about making a sequal or new version of arras.io, this idea would eventually become the unity game known as Talaris about 9 months later. During the discussions and plans for this game in early 2020, multiple projects had been thrown into the topic, Dooky suggested making a brand or company to give the projects an identity of who made them. After some thought, Diroga (die-rouge-a) was concieved. Though the idea of creating a company would fall silent for a good 6 months, 立MEGA VIRUS eventually came back to the idea and created a logo, discord, and website for Diroga. Games are now being produced under the name to this day.

About Exerras.io

Posted November 12, 2020

In this section we'll learn a bit about Diroga's first game Exerras.io

立MEGA VIRUS, the main founder of Diroga, is the creator of the IO game known as Exerras.io. arras.io was an IO game created to be an unofficial sequal to diep.io, most of the code for arras.io became open source for anybody to make their own servers. 立MEGA VIRUS used this code to start his own server on 1/20/20, it has grown exponentially since then. After he started investing most of his time on other projects and eventually building up Diroga, Exerras.io got a co-owner who goes by Stage 7 Depression.

Exerras.io is a web based game, so as long as you have an internet connection, you can play it. The main goal of the game is to kill other players and gain score using a simple geometric style tank. You can upgrade your tank by selecting a tank from the upgrade menu in the top left of the screen. Theres 4 tiers of upgrades, and a possible 24 tanks per upgrade, that means its possible to have 331,776 tanks, though its only a little over 400 at the moment. You can increase your stats by using the little menu in the bottom left corner. You have 45 stat upgrade points, meaning you can fully fill out up to 5 stats such as bullet reload, tank movement speed, bullet damage, etc.
